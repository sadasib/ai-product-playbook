# AI Product Playbook

> A practical operating system for building, evaluating, and launching AI products responsibly.

Building AI products requires more than writing prompts or shipping models. Successful AI products combine customer understanding, rigorous evaluation, responsible AI practices, operational excellence, and measurable business outcomes.

This repository brings together practical frameworks, templates, and examples that help AI Product Managers move from **idea в†’ evaluation в†’ launch** with confidence.

# The 5 Gates of AI Product Launch

Every AI product should pass five gates before reaching production.

| Gate                           | Key Question                                          | Primary Artifacts                        |
| ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **G1 вЂ” Customer Value**        | Are we solving a meaningful customer problem?         | AI PRD                                   |
| **G2 вЂ” AI Quality**            | Is the AI consistently good enough?                   | Evaluation Plan, AI Evaluation Workbench |
| **G3 вЂ” Trust & Safety**        | Can customers trust the system?                       | Risk Register                            |
| **G4 вЂ” Operational Readiness** | Can the organization support and monitor the feature? | Launch Readiness Framework               |
| **G5 вЂ” Business Readiness**    | Does the product create measurable business value?    | Executive Decision Memo                  |

> Every artifact in this repository maps to one or more of these five gates.

# Design Principles

This playbook is built around a few simple principles:

* Start with the customer problemвЂ”not the model.
* Define quality before writing prompts.
* Evaluate using real-world and edge-case scenarios.
* Treat Responsible AI as a product requirement.
* Launch only when customer value, AI quality, operational readiness, and business outcomes are aligned.
* Learn continuously after launch.
