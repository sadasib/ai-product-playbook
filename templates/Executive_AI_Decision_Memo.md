# Executive AI Launch Decision Memo

## Executive Summary

### Feature

Retail Returns Assistant

### Decision Requested

Approve progression from Beta to General Availability (GA).

### Recommendation

**Recommendation: Proceed with General Availability**

The product has met predefined launch thresholds across AI quality, customer value, safety, and operational readiness. Remaining risks are understood and mitigated through monitoring and human escalation workflows.

---

# Business Context

## Problem

Customers frequently contact support with questions regarding return eligibility, refund timelines, and return policies, increasing support costs and resolution time.

## Expected Business Impact

* Reduce customer support contacts
* Improve self-service completion
* Increase customer satisfaction
* Lower average handling time
* Improve operational efficiency
